Welcome to my Website!

I am a 20 year old university student at JU and studying New media design. I grew up in the southern edge of the Stockholm province and graduated highschool in 2021, after which i moved to Jönköping to study AI engineering here at JU. I realised quickly however that it did not suit me and i applied to the nmd program.

I did a lot of graphic design and photography in my spare time when i was younger, it has been a hobby of mine for 8+ years and now it's something i want to turn into a career. Other hobbies of mine include cooking, video games, biking, being in nature.
