Jönköping

Here in Jönköping you can do all kinds of things, like for example visit one of the many churches the city has to offer, as well as go on long walks along the beach. There are also multiple different bars and restaurants you can visit here as well.

    Places to visit:

  • Kappa bar
  • Asecs
  • Sofiakyrkan
  • Rådhusparken
  • Kulturhuset spira

    Some good places to eat include:

  • Sushi Yama
  • Bastard Burgers
  • Pizzeria Campino
  • Mr Rice
